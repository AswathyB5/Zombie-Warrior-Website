Death Warrior


Death Warrior is a fearless fighter born in a world overrun by the undead. He hunts zombies with his cursed blade, forged to destroy the walking dead. Once a lone soldier, now a legend feared by monsters and praised by survivors. His mission: cleanse the land and end the zombie plague once and for all.

Backstory: Death Warrior

Once a revered general of the Shadow Legion, he was betrayed in battle and left to rot beneath the cursed soil of the Blackmoor Valley. Years later, dark magic reawakened his corpse — not with vengeance, but with a relentless hunger to destroy the undead who now plague the realm. Bound by rune-forged chains and driven by forgotten honor, he wanders the wastelands, his eyes glowing with the power of corrupted souls. He is no longer man, nor monster — he is the Death Warrior.
