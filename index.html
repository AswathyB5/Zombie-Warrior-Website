Backstory: Zombie Warrior

Once a revered general of the Shadow Legion, he was betrayed in battle and left to rot beneath the cursed soil of the Blackmoor Valley. Years later, dark magic reawakened his corpse — not with vengeance, but with a relentless hunger to destroy the undead who now plague the realm. Bound by rune-forged chains and driven by forgotten honor, he wanders the wastelands, his eyes glowing with the power of corrupted souls. He is no longer man, nor monster — he is the Zombie Warrior.
